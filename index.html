Tiny the Burrito Pup Chihuahua

Tiny is a lover, not a fighter. A defender of justice. A wiggle butt. An all-around loveable dog.

He has quite a few unique quirks!

  • Usually one ear sticks up straight while the other flops
  • He has white mittens
  • His shoulder blades have a smattering of white hair creating the illusion of wings
  • He loves to chase the laser pointer
  • He loves to lick and clean his paws

It is important to train your dog for both animal and owner!

Trick Description Mastered Performs without Dog Treat Bribe
